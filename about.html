Our Story

About FreeLegalTools

We're not lawyers. We're everyday people who got tired of legal questions having no clear answers — and decided to build the tools we wished existed.

"FreeLegalTools.com was born out of pure frustration."

Like most people, we've faced legal questions at the worst possible times — after a job loss, before signing a contract, or when starting a new business venture. And like most people, we turned to Google for answers.

What we found was overwhelming. Expensive lawyers. Confusing legal jargon. Websites that buried simple answers behind paywalls and account signups. Plenty of information for legal professionals — almost nothing for regular people who just needed a straight answer.

So we built the tools we wished existed.

FreeLegalTools.com is not run by lawyers. We're everyday people who believe that basic legal information should be free, simple, and accessible to everyone — not just those who can afford an attorney.

Every tool on this site is designed with one person in mind — someone who needs a clear answer right now, in plain English, at no cost. That's it. That's our whole mission.
