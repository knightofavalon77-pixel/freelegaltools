Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here's exactly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

The Short Version
  • ✓We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal information to use our tools
  • ✓No account or registration is ever required
  • ✓We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site (anonymized data only)

Section 1
Who We Are

FreeLegalTools.com ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at www.freelegaltools.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle information when you visit our site.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.

Section 2
Information We Collect

We collect two types of information: information you voluntarily provide and information collected automatically.

Information you provide: When you use our contact form, you provide your name, email address, and message. We use this information only to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list or share this information with third parties.

Information collected automatically: When you visit our site, certain information is collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies, including:

  • Pages you visit and how long you spend on them
  • Your general geographic location (country/city level — not precise location)
  • The type of device, browser, and operating system you are using
  • How you arrived at our site (search engine, direct link, etc.)
  • Your IP address (anonymized before storage)

We do not collect, store, or process any of the answers you enter into our legal tools. Your tool inputs and results are processed entirely in your browser and are never transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Section 3
How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it
  • To respond to messages submitted through our contact form
  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To monitor site performance and fix technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, do not sell it to data brokers, and do not share it with third parties except as described in this policy.

Section 4
Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. We use cookies for the following purposes:

Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors find and use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymized data about page views, session duration, and traffic sources. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our site. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies to show you ads based on your interests and your prior visits to this and other websites. These are third-party cookies placed by Google, not by FreeLegalTools.com directly.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of our site or the relevance of advertisements you see.

Section 5
Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this site. Google AdSense is a service provided by Google LLC. Google uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising in the following ways:

  • Visit Google's Ads Settings to opt out of personalized ads from Google
  • Visit AboutAds.info to opt out of interest-based advertising from participating companies
  • Use your browser's private/incognito mode
  • Adjust your browser's cookie settings

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our site, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

Section 6
Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about your visit:

  • Google Analytics — website analytics. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography. Fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address. Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — website hosting. Your IP address and request data may be logged by our hosting provider. Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Section 7
Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for a reasonable period thereafter for record-keeping purposes, after which they are deleted.

Analytics data collected by Google Analytics is retained according to Google's standard data retention settings (26 months by default). You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

As noted above, tool inputs and results are never stored on our servers — they exist only in your browser during your session.

Section 8
Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising
  • The right to data portability (where applicable)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe. Please note that some information may need to be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

Section 9
Children's Privacy

FreeLegalTools.com is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete that information.

Section 10
Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of FreeLegalTools.com after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Section 11
Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us through our contact form. We take privacy seriously and will respond promptly to your inquiry.
